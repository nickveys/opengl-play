Usage

To create a bezier curve, click New Curve and start adding points. Once there are at least 3 points the curve will become visible. Continued clicking adds more points.

To modify a curve, click Modify Points and click-drag points to change the curve.

To create a bezier surface, click New Surface and one will be created.

To rotate the view, click Camera Model and click-drag to change the view on the curve/surface.

To change U, slide the U spinner from 0 to 1.

Known Issues

  • Manual creation of surfaces is not implemented. One has its control points statically defined.
  • Shaded surfaces are not implemented. The quad strip is complete, but no normals are specified.
  • Camera rotation is off-axis. I was unable to see why exactly, as it is a direct use of the camera class as before.
  • Surfaces are not modifyable.
