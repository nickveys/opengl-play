File Descriptions

Camera.{h,cpp}
Class implementing the core functionality of the camera
Object.h
Base class for shapes that can be added to the screen, extensions are contained in here as well
Matrix.{h,cpp}
Matrix class with some modification (documented)
Group.h
Object extension holding multiple objects
Root.h
Specialized Group, acts as the 'world'
common.h
Simple common defines and structs
main.cpp
Entry point, initializes, creates UI

Camera Controls

  • Rotate - Click and drag to rotate the view
  • Twist - Click and drag left and right to twist left and right
  • Zoom - Click and drag up and down to zoom in and out
  • Pan - Click and drag to pan the view

Known Issues

  • The View All seems a bit liberal in its zooming, there is lots of blank area around, it may be due to the sizing of the objects, I tried to keep them at or near 1.0 radius, and some may be smaller so the view all gives them more space.
